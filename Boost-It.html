Welcome to Boost-It

Try your best to survive in space as you fly around dangerous obstacles in your attempt to make it to safety!

(NOTE: Boost-It is best experienced on desktop or laptop since it is not a mobile game.)

Play Boost-It

Boost-It was created with C# and built on the Unity Engine. Boost-It is a fun physics-based game where you fly a rocket to specific locations on the map while trying to avoid obstacles. Through out the evolution of each level, new obstacles and mechanisms are introduced to give the players more of a challenge. I truly had a blast creating Boost-It so I hope you enjoy it as well!
